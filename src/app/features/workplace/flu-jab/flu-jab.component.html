Why do we want to know?

DHSC want to know how many workers will be protected from flu this winter. This information could tell us that 60% of care workers in a certain area are vaccinated. This will help identify any extra local demands on the NHS and the care sector. It could also highlight a need for better promotion of the vaccine within the care sector. Your data will not be shared with any other organisation.
