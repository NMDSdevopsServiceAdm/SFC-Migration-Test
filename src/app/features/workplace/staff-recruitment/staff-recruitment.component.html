Staff Recruitment

We've added 4 new staff recruitment questions to ASC-WDS. We want to know:

  • how much money is being spent on advertising for staff?
  • how many people are being interviewed?
  • whether new starters have to repeat training done with previous employers?
  • whether Care Certificates are transferrable from workplace to workplace?

Why are we collecting this data?

The Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC) want to use our anonymised data to help them better understand the difficulties and cost of recruitment into the adult social care sector.

Your information will help DHSC to identify where policy changes could make staff recruitment easier in the future.
